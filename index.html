Msc Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence

At a glance

The MSc in Data Analytics & Artificial Intelligence will hone your business decisionmaking skills. You will acquire a “common language" understandable by data scientists, developers, and managers to create insightful analysis and innovative strategies for businesses in the digital age. The programme prepares you for a wide range of positions and opportunities where programming, quantitative analysis, critical thinking and business management skills are essential.

Program Director: Mr Florian Pelgrin

“I am excited by the MSc in Data Analytics & Artificial Intelligence and its ability to make the experience of learning exciting, cutting edge and meaningful to the business challenges confronting the world today. The development of this MSc programme is the cultivation of my, 20-years of experience as an educator and practitioner. It fulfils my purpose to create a platform to educate the brightest minds interested in data sciences and business analytics. I chose to create the MSc in Data Analytics & Artificial Intelligence at EDHEC because the spirit of this business school corresponds to the mission and values of our programme: to bridge the gap between data specialists, IT managers and decision makers and build the new generation of managers able to create successful and innovative strategies for the digital age. I am thrilled to help shape the future of our industry.”
